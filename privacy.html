Data We Collect

We only access your Gmail account through the gmail.settings.basic and gmail.readonly scopes to identify lead notification emails from Zillow, Redfin, and Realtor.com.

How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to provide the Flux Vision lead-routing service. This includes:

Lead Triage Extracting contact info from Zillow emails.

Security Standards

All authentication is handled via OAuth 2.0. Flux Automation never sees or stores your Google password. We utilize AES-256 encryption for all data in transit.
