Privacy & Data Governance

Effective Date: March 31, 2026. Based in New Jersey, USA.

Google API Limited Use Disclosure

"Flux Automation's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements."

Data Collection Scope

We only access your Gmail account through the gmail.settings.basic and gmail.readonly scopes to identify lead notification emails from Zillow, Redfin, and Realtor.com.

  • We do NOT read personal or non-real-estate correspondence.
  • We do NOT store your full email bodies on permanent storage.
  • We do NOT sell or share lead data with third-party advertisers.

Operational Usage

Data is used exclusively to provide the Flux Vision lead-routing service:

Lead Triage Automated parsing of Zillow alerts.
Instant Delivery Encrypted SMS/WhatsApp notifications.

Encryption Standards

Authentication is handled via OAuth 2.0. We never see or store your Google password. We utilize AES-256 encryption for all data in transit between your account and our server at connect.fluxautomation.site.
